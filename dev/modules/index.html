    ElectromagneticFields.SymmetricQuadratic — Type.

    Symmetric quadratic equilibrium in (x,y,z) coordinates.

    \[B(x,y,z) = B_0 \, \begin{pmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \\ 1 + x^2 + y^2 \\ \end{pmatrix}\]

    with electric field

    \[E(x,y,z) = E_0 \, \begin{pmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \\ \cos (2\pi z) \\ \end{pmatrix}\]

    Parameters: B₀, E₀
